## Bio

I am a computational scientist working hard to positively impact human health. I am unusually interdisciplinary, having developed deep expertise in biology, functional genomics, human genetics, and machine learning. I am most productive when combining these.

In the past: 
* I have led a machine learning team at [Altos Labs](https://altoslabs.com) in its Institute of Computation, developing large language models for epigenetics. 
* I identified novel targets for therapeutic intervention in SLE at [Genomics](https://genomics.com) and developed complete therapeutic stories for them through the combination of skills above.
    * I have returned for a short stint Genomics to build their gene expression platform in [Mystra](https://mystra.com/), and prototype AI agent-based access and analysis of their databases.
* I pioneered the first analyses of large-scale single-cell RNA-sequencing data during my PhD in John Marioni's lab.
